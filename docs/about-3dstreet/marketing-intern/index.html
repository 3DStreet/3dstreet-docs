We're Hiring: Marketing & Product Marketing Intern (Summer 2026)

Temporary posting

This is a short-term opening. Remote (Pacific time zone), paid at a basic undergrad student summer intern rate, for up to 2 months, starting within two weeks.

About 3DStreet​

3DStreet is a tool for designing streets in 3D. Urban planners, students, advocates, and city staff use it to visualize better streets and share them. Our users build scenes that reimagine their streets, and we want your help telling those stories.

The role​

We are hiring a Marketing & Product Marketing Intern for Summer 2026. We want someone to focus on marketing and product marketing, mostly by making videos and using the app. You already use 3DStreet, you make things, and you can turn a good scene into something worth watching. This is not a sales role (we tried that last summer). It is about showing what the product can do and helping to build an audience around it.

What you'll do:

  • Make short videos for social (LinkedIn, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Discord, etc.)
  • Feature great user scenes and turn them into content
  • Use the app hands-on to demo features and keep a steady posting rhythm
  • Help shape how we talk about new features as they launch

What you need​

  • You use 3DStreet and have made more than one scene. You can share one as part of your application.
  • You have made videos for social media before. Any platform / type is ok.

Nice to have​

An interest in urban planning and social media, plus some comfort with creative tools like Illustrator, Photoshop, SketchUp, or 3DStreet. We are not picky about which. We just want to see that you like making things.

Details​

  • Location: Remote, Pacific time zone
  • Pay: Paid, modest but real
  • Duration: Up to 2 months
  • Start: Within two weeks

How to apply​

Make a video. Show off one of your streets, that is it!

  • No longer than 30 seconds
  • Feature a scene you built in 3DStreet
  • Make it something you would actually want to post

Send the video to team@3dstreet.com and we will see you in the next step.
