Introducing Pro Team: Simple, Affordable Site Licenses for Organizations and Universities

October 24, 2025 · 4 min read
Kieran Farr
Creator of 3DStreet

I'm excited to announce that our Pro Team plan is now available for organizations and universities everywhere. After years of work with public agencies, universities, and non-profit organizations, I've heard the same request over and over: make it simple to get 3DStreet Pro access for an entire team or classroom.

Today we made it happen -- for just $5,000 per year, your organization gets Pro access for up to 50 users with a straightforward activation process.
